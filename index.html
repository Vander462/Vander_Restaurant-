History

Vander Restaurant began as a small eatery with just a few tables and a passionate team of chefs. The founder, Chef Elena Martinez, dreamed of a place where every dish tells a story. Inspired by her grandmother's recipes, she focused on using high-quality, seasonal ingredients. As word spread about the mouthwatering flavors and inviting atmosphere, Vander quickly grew in popularity.

Values

At the core of Vander's philosophy is a commitment to sustainability and community. The restaurant partners with local farmers and artisans, ensuring that every ingredient used is not only fresh but also supports the local economy. Vander is dedicated to reducing its carbon footprint, using eco-friendly practices in both the kitchen and dining areas.

Specialties

Vander is particularly famous for its signature dish: the "Mutton Rogan Josh," served with seasonal vegetables and a rich red wine reduction. Another crowd favorite is the "Chicken Curry," which embodies the essence of comfort food with a gourmet twist. For dessert, the "Chocolate Lava Cake" is a must-try, offering a perfect ending to any meal.
